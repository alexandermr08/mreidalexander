First stop: www.codeacademy.com

Codeacademy was mentioned in the article so it was where I began my programming journey. While I found it to be helpful in getting my feet wet and introducing me to programming, I knew I needed more if I were going to make it a career...

Enter: www.W3schools.com & YouTube

After spending time completing courses on Codeacademy, I found W3schools and YouTube channels dedicated to teaching entry level programming. I now use YouTube for lecture, W3schools as my text, and codeacademy serves as my exercises to practice and internalize the material. I finally feel that I am making progress and these three resources are what allowed me to get this site online.
