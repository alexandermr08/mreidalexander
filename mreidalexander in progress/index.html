Hi, I'm Reid Alexander, a self taught web developer.

The inspiration...

As long as I can remember I have known I wanted to be an entrepreneur. To be involved in something from its conception and to mold the way a company conducts business is my dream.

As a result, I have spent a fair amount of time researching the necessary skills to be successful as an entrepreneur. One day I stumbled across an article mentioning the benefit of knowing how to code for young entrepreneurs and it was like a light bulb came on in my head. This was something I could use to make my dream a reality...

Everything you see here is a product of my journey.
